ART RANCH

About: Art Ranch is a non-profit art education organization located in the California Hot Springs. In the summer of 2011, I managed a team of UCLA architecture students to design a series of tents, cabins, and open spaces for the future art camp for kids. These designs were used to showcase to potential donors of the ranch.

Skills: AutoCAD, Rhinoceros, Adobe Creative Suite, VRay Rendering

Team: Daniel Nguyen, Lillian Tara Zeinalzadegan, Anne, and Loosine Vartani
