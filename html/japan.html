JAPAN PHOTO JOURNAL

About: This map traces back the footsteps taken during my 2014 Japan trip with the UCLA School of Public Policy. The trip took place in Tohoku, Toyko, and Kyoto. In Tohoku, we looked at the disaster relief efforts after the 2011 Tsunami. We observed the reconstruction in the region as well as the city planning initiatives to house the 500,000 displaced local population.

Skills: Picasa API, Google Maps

Data: Geographic data from iPhoto library
